<h2>Controls</h2>
- MOUSE = pan around
- ENTER = play sorting animation
- LEFT_ARROW = step backwards
- RIGHT_ARROW = step forwards
- SCROLL_UP = zoom in
- SCROLL_DOWN = zoom out
